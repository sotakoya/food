The Menu @ RAPSA!



Get ready to indulge in a culinary adventure at RAPSA! Our menu is bursting with flavor, featuring a delightful array of tacos, katsu, lumpia, and so much more. Whether you're in the mood for a taste of the Philippines or craving some Mexican-inspired goodness, we've got something to delight your taste buds. Swing by and join us for a meal that's sure to put a smile on your face!
Appetizers Entrées Gourmet Tacos Quesadilla / Quesabirria / Loaded Fries
Pork/Chicken Lumpia

Indulge in our irresistible lumpia! Made with a savory blend of ground chicken or pork, mixed veggies, and expert seasoning, each roll is fried to crispy perfection. Don't forget to dip it in sweet chili sauce, included for the perfect finishing touch!

Beef/Chicken Empanadas

Savor our savory empanadas! Ground chicken or beef mixed with potatoes, carrots, raisins, sweet peas, and bell peppers, all cooked in tomato sauce, then stuffed in our homemade empanada dough. Available deep-fried or baked (catering).

Elote in a Bowl

Enjoy a bowl of sweet corn topped with creamy mayonnaise, tangy Mexican sour cream, flavorful garlic seasoning, crumbled Cotija cheese, zesty Tajin, fresh cilantro, and a splash of lime. It's a deliciously vibrant combination you'll love!

Crunchy Shrimp Tostada

Get ready to savor every bite of our Crunchy Shrimp Tostadas, featuring creamy mayo, our very own Mango Salsa, crumbled Cotija Cheese, succulent grilled shrimp, and a finishing touch of fresh cilantro. It's a culinary adventure you'll want to dive into again and again!

Pork Adobo/Chicken Enchilada

Treat yourself to our delectable pork adobo or chicken enchilada, made with our exclusive Masa dough and packed with flavorful Shredded Pork Adobo or Chicken. It's a culinary experience like no other!

Fresh Lumpia

Indulge in our fresh lumpia, filled with sautéed carrots, green beans, bean sprouts, water chestnuts, and tofu. Wrapped in a homemade crepe and crisp lettuce, and topped with sweet garlic sauce and chopped peanuts. It's a delightful combination you won't want to miss!

Pork Belly BBQ

Savor our mouthwatering pork belly BBQ, marinated in a flavorful blend of soy sauce, lemon, fresh garlic, and select seasonings. Enjoy it served with steamed rice, sweet corn, and tangy kimchi for a deliciously satisfying meal!

Grilled Chicken BBQ & Shrimp Teriyaki

Try our Grilled Chicken BBQ and Shrimp Teriyaki! Our Boneless/Skinless Chicken Thighs are marinated in soy sauce, lemon, garlic, and seasoning, paired with Grilled Shrimp in teriyaki sauce. Served with steamed rice, sweet corn, and kimchi.
